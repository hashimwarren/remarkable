# Complete the Ending

Finish the movement of the premise rather than mechanically summarizing the article. Choose the fitting mode:

- **Consequence:** deepen what follows if the premise is true.
- **Resolution:** return to and resolve a tension from the opening.
- **Transfer:** move the insight into the reader's world.
- **Action:** give the natural next step.

Ask one question when guidance is needed:

> How should the reader's belief, decision, or behavior be different when the article ends?

When a commercial call to action belongs, evaluate the offer with RUVU:

- **Risk-free:** Is hesitation reduced credibly?
- **Unique:** Is the next step meaningfully differentiated?
- **Valuable:** Is the benefit worth the reader's effort or cost?
- **Urgent:** Is there a truthful reason to act now?

Identify when the problem is the offer rather than CTA wording. Never invent scarcity, deadlines, risk reversal, or urgency. Remove the CTA section when the article has not earned one.
